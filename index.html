Dashboard Help & Field Guide
Live Price: latest quote fetched through Yahoo-style market data logic.
Day Move: daily price change versus the previous close or reference price.
Allocation: portfolio weight for each ticker. Larger allocations affect portfolio scores more.
Trend: 0–100 momentum score. Higher means stronger technical trend.
Fundamentals: 0–100 business or asset quality score.
Valuation: 0–100 attractiveness score. Higher means more reasonable valuation.
Risk: 0–100 score where higher means lower perceived risk.
Sentiment: 0–100 market/news/analyst perception score.
Macro: 0–100 broader economic backdrop score.
Expected Return: user assumption used for portfolio and risk/return charts.
Final Score: weighted composite score for each ticker.
Best-Fit Line: shows whether expected return appears to rise or fall as risk changes.
