Quick Reference
Yahoo updates Live Price and Day Move. Trend, Fundamentals, Valuation, Risk, Sentiment, Macro, and Expected Return are research assumptions that the user can edit.

Portfolio Quality

Allocation-weighted confidence score across holdings.

Risk Rating

Higher risk score means lower perceived risk.

Diversification

Measures concentration across allocations.

Expected Return

Allocation-weighted return assumption, not a guarantee.

Columns, controls, and charts
  • Ticker: Yahoo Finance symbol. Select an existing ticker or type any new symbol.
  • Live Price: Latest quote returned from Yahoo-compatible chart routes.
  • Day Move: Current price change from Yahoo quote data.
  • Allocation %: Portfolio weight. It drives portfolio-level scores and allocation chart.
  • Trend: Momentum score from 0–100. Higher means stronger price momentum.
  • Fund.: Fundamental quality score covering growth, margins, cash flow, leverage, and moat.
  • Valuation: Higher means the asset appears more reasonably valued under the user's assumptions.
  • Risk: Higher is better; higher means lower perceived risk.
  • Sent.: Market/news/analyst/institutional sentiment score.
  • Macro: Interest-rate, inflation, sector, and economic backdrop score.
  • Exp. Ret. %: Expected return assumption used in scenario and risk/return analysis.
  • Final: 25% Fund. + 20% Trend + 20% Valuation + 15% Sent. + 10% Macro + 10% Risk.
  • Risk vs Return: Shows expected return against risk score. The best-fit line interprets whether return assumptions rise or fall with risk.
